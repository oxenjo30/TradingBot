# PrimusTrader — Changelog

Customer-facing release notes. Each entry summarizes what changed in that version
in plain language. (Internal/infrastructure changes are omitted.)

## v1.5 — Purchasing & delivery
- Buy PrimusTrader on either **Whop** or **Gumroad** — pick your preferred store.
- Instant delivery: your license key and a secure download link arrive by email the
  moment your payment completes.
- License-delivery emails now come from the official PrimusTrader address.

## v1.1 — One-click updates
- The dashboard can now check for and install updates with a single click — no
  manual reinstall. Your settings, accounts, and history are preserved across updates.

## v1.0 — Initial release
- Automated algorithmic trading for stocks and crypto across your connected broker
  accounts.
- 13 built-in strategies on a 60-second engine tick, multi-layer risk controls, and
  AI trade explanations — all from one dashboard.
- Backtesting, performance tracking, and a weekly auto-tuner.
