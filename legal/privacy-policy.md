# Privacy Policy

**Product:** PrimusTrader — Self-Hosted Algorithmic Trading Bot
**Seller:** PrimusTrader ("we", "us", "our")
**Contact:** kurtis@primustrader.com
**Last updated:** June 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information we handle when you buy and use PrimusTrader (the "Software"). The most important point: **PrimusTrader is self-hosted, so your trading data and credentials stay on your own computer.**

## 1. Information we receive

**At purchase (via Whop):** When you buy through Whop, Whop collects and processes your payment and contact details (such as your name and email) under Whop's own privacy policy. We receive limited order information from Whop — typically your email address and order ID — so we can deliver your license key and provide support.

**When you contact us:** If you email us for support, we receive the information you choose to send (such as your email address and the contents of your message).

## 2. Information we do NOT receive

Because the Software runs entirely on your machine, **we do not collect, receive, store, or have access to**:
- Your broker or exchange API keys or secrets.
- Your trading account balances, positions, orders, or history.
- Your strategy configurations or dashboard password.
- Any Claude API key you choose to add.

This data is stored locally on your computer. Your broker credentials and other secrets are encrypted at rest on your machine and are never transmitted to us.

## 3. How we use information

We use the limited information we receive (your email and order details) only to:
- Deliver your license key and product files.
- Provide customer support and updates.
- Comply with our legal and tax obligations.

We do not sell your personal information.

## 4. Third-party services

The Software connects to third parties **you choose** (e.g., Alpaca, Tradier, Binance, and optionally Anthropic's Claude API). Those connections happen directly from your computer to those services and are governed by their privacy policies, not ours. Payments are handled by Whop under Whop's privacy policy.

## 5. Data retention

We retain order and support records only as long as needed to provide support, deliver updates, and meet legal/accounting requirements, after which we delete or anonymize them.

## 6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you (such as your email and order record). To make a request, email kurtis@primustrader.com.

## 7. Children

The Software is not intended for anyone under 18 (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

## 8. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the current version.
