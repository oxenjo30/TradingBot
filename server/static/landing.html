Risk management

9 guards between you
and a bad trade

Every signal — from strategies or webhooks — passes through all 9 guards before an order is placed.

Kill Switch
One click halts all automated trading instantly. Global or per-account.
Daily Loss Limit
Auto-activates kill switch when portfolio drops below your threshold.
Weekly Loss Limit
Halts trading for the week when cumulative P&L drops past your weekly threshold — a second safety net beyond the daily limit.
PDT Protection
Tracks day-trade count and blocks a 4th trade automatically on sub-$25K accounts.
Max Positions
Caps total open positions — separate limits for stocks and crypto.
Symbol Exposure
Prevents allocating more than N% of portfolio to a single symbol.
Take-Profit
Auto-closes positions when they hit your target gain percentage.
Consecutive Loss Guard
Pauses trading after N losses in a row until you manually review.
No-Trade List
Blacklist any symbol permanently — never traded regardless of signals.
